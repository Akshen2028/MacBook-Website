  1. Microsoft
    • Great apple competitor in the current generation and manufactures PC’s, laptops, mobile phones and software systems.
    • Microsoft windows operating systems are a huge competitor the the Mac operating system that apple MacBooks offer.
    • The microsoft Surface Book is able to work well with iTunes, iCloud, and the iPhone, which makes it even easier for users to switch from a macbook to a surface book.
  2. Dell
    • Known for the manufacturing of laptops and mobile PC’s.
    • Dell does not have smartphones and is one of Apple’s competitors with specifically laptops.
    • Dell laptops such as the Dell XPS 13 contains key features comparable to apple MacBooks at a cheaper price point.
  3. Lenovo
    • Chinese company that manufactures consumer PCs, mobile phones, and consumer electronics along with software systems.
    • A top competitor for apple in all aspects of technology including smartphones, Laptops and Tablets.
    • Lenovo has an excellent range of laptops at good prices, along with a very diversified product portfolio.
